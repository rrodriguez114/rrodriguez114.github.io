keybored

Keybored is a web toy inspired by midi controllers such as the mpc and launchpad. These controllers allow you to create music by triggering audio samples by pressingon one of their pads. The goal of Keybored was to mimick this experience using the keys on your computers keyboard. Rather than press pads on a controller, Keybored allows you to trigger pre-loaded samples by typing any of the keys that make up its 4x4 grid.
